Google I/O

Google I/O is two days of inspirational talks, hands-on learning, and a chance to hear more about Google's latest developer products.

Who Should Attend?

Developers, designers, students, project managers, CEOs, and anyone else interested in the latest product innovation from Google.

What to Expect?

Day one will open with a keynote address broadcast live from Google in Melbourne, Australia. Breakout sessions begin after the first meal break and continue for the next two days.
